## Selection Notes

All eleven creators were chosen because they are **practitioners first** — each either runs a YouTube channel or LinkedIn presence that drives real business outcomes (leads, sales, acquisitions, pipeline) for a SaaS or SaaS-adjacent company, or has documented, transparent frameworks for how they produce that content. The mix intentionally covers multiple angles of the same topic:

- **YouTube SEO and search-driven discovery:** Samu Kovács, Exposure Ninja
- **Founder-led content as a growth lever:** Alex Heiden, Pat Walls / Starter Story
- **SaaS-specific frameworks and authority-building:** Rob Walling, TK Kader
- **Messaging and conversion-focused scripting:** Joanna Wiebe
- **Content strategy, planning, and prioritization for B2B SaaS:** Brendan Hufford, Pierre Herubel
- **Editorial craft and storytelling for content:** Tommy Walker
- **Building an owned audience and turning trust into pipeline:** Devin Reed

This combination should give a well-rounded foundation for a real B2B SaaS YouTube content playbook — covering strategy, production systems, messaging, and the broader content-to-pipeline thinking that LinkedIn-native creators bring to the table, not just one narrow tactic.
